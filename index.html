About Me

I am an engineer, researcher, and innovator with expertise in control systems, aerospace technology, and robotics. My work spans advanced UAV designs, CubeSat development, and groundbreaking research in resistojet propulsion and lunar landing technologies. Passionate about pushing the boundaries of engineering, I strive to contribute to cutting-edge solutions in automation and sustainability.

Projects

Variable Swept Wing UAV

Developed a dynamic wing mechanism to enhance UAV stability and efficiency, integrating sensor feedback for real-time adjustments.

Earth Observational Satellite (EOSAT)

Engineered a CubeSat for Earth observation, optimizing its structure, thermal analysis, and power system design using solar panels.

Resistojet Propulsion System

Led the development of an advanced resistojet propulsion system, achieving a 4% efficiency improvement through optimized material selection and design.

Underwater Autonomous Vehicle

Collaborating on an underwater vehicle with CURC, focusing on SLAM integration and depth camera technology for enhanced navigation.

Fourier Transform Profilometry for Lunar Landings

Developing FTP technology as an alternative to LiDAR, improving navigation and accuracy for lunar landing missions in challenging environments.

Advanced Drone Cargo Delivery

Designed and tested a drone for cargo delivery, achieving double the required payload capacity during the Smart Drone Event.
